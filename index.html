This is Me!

This is page is all about me. I am to explain who I am and convince you to hire me.

I was born in the south of Nigeria. I was raised by my grandparents who emphasized the importance of science and technology in the lives. My grandfather would buy the newest computers and show me how to you them. This was how my love for computers began. My first device was a Samsung SPH A680, I cherished my phone but also used it to take pictures and play video games.

15 years later, I am learning to build software that run on mobile and Web. It is such a privilege and also a challenge that I am adjusting to. I would like to build a software to facilitate trade between African Clothing Designers and the rest of the world. An app like Poshmark but for hardworking people who do not have an avenue to display and sell their art. It will also be an app for for people in developed countries to purchase and wear cultural attires.
